Defaults are conservative by design. HR lags power by 30–60 s. With a 30 s tick and 10 W/tick cap, the system makes one adjustment and waits a full lag period before the next — preventing the overshoot seen with faster settings. Only tighten if steady-state tracking is too slow.

Kd = 0 recommended. Derivative is computed on HR, which is already 30–60 s stale at tick time — it amplifies noise more than it damps. Only add Kd (try 0.5) if you see persistent limit-cycle oscillation.

Changing target mid-session resets the integral automatically so windup from the old target doesn't corrupt the new one.
